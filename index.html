Archivus: An AI Assitant for Researchers

Working with health and human performance scientists as a Data Analyst, I've had the chance to see all of the grunt work that underlies high-quality, academic research. Many researchers in this field and others manually take down data by hand, and then must manually enter all of it into a spreadsheet.

To make their life easier, I am currently developing Archivus: an AI model that will allow researchers to upload pictures of handwritten data, and then convert it to spreadsheet format automatically. With regard to both numeric and English character recognition, my model is currently at 95% accuracy.

I am working on refining its ability to detect table structure and accurately replicate it. I estimate that I will complete this within the next month or two.
